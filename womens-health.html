MTHFR & Pregnancy

The folate gene that every woman planning pregnancy needs to know about

MTHFR variants affect how efficiently your body converts folate into its active form — 5-methyltetrahydrofolate. If you carry the C677T or A1298C variants, standard folic acid supplements may not provide adequate protection during early pregnancy.

Kyronix checks your MTHFR genotype and provides specific guidance on supplementation forms, dosing, and dietary sources — personalized to your exact variant combination.

Perimenopause Detection

Catch the transition early — before symptoms take over

Perimenopause begins years before the last period — and the data changes often show up before the symptoms do. Irregular cycles, subtle HRV shifts, sleep fragmentation, and rising FSH levels are all early indicators that Kyronix can detect and contextualize.

Knowing you're in early perimenopause changes the conversation with your doctor — and gives you a head start on the lifestyle and hormonal strategies that make the transition smoother.
