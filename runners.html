Injury Risk Intelligence

COL5A1 — Your connective tissue blueprint

COL5A1 is the gene that encodes type V collagen — the structural protein that forms tendons and ligaments. Certain variants are associated with elevated risk of Achilles tendon and ACL injury.

Kyronix flags your COL5A1 genotype and adjusts training load recommendations accordingly — so you can push hard without walking the line between PR and injury.

Combined with your weekly mileage, training load trends, and sleep recovery data, Kyronix gives you an injury risk score that's actually personalized to your biology.
