Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 7, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 7, 2026

Kyronix ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Kyronix mobile application and website (kyronix.health).

1. Information We Collect

Kyronix may collect the following categories of data:

  • Health & Activity Data — steps, heart rate, sleep, workouts, HRV, body composition, and other metrics you authorize through Apple HealthKit or connected wearable devices (Oura, Garmin).
  • Lab Results — manually entered lab values and results imported from PDF documents or Apple Health Records (EHR data from Epic, Cerner, and other providers).
  • Genetic Data — raw DNA files (AncestryDNA, 23andMe, VCF) you voluntarily upload. Genetic data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Profile Information — age, sex, height, weight, and other profile fields you optionally provide.
  • Usage Data — anonymous crash reports and feature usage analytics used to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate personalized health insights and AI-powered analysis via our Companion feature.
  • To display your health trends, lab results, and activity summaries within the app.
  • To produce PDF health reports you can share with your healthcare providers.
  • To improve app functionality and fix bugs.

We do not sell your personal health data to third parties. We do not use your health data for advertising.

3. Activity Data from Wearable Devices

If you connect a wearable device (such as a Garmin or Oura device), Kyronix receives activity data including steps, heart rate, sleep metrics, GPS-based distance, calories burned, and related fitness metrics. This data is:

  • Used solely to power your personal health dashboard and AI insights.
  • Stored locally on your device and, where applicable, within your iCloud account.
  • Never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.
  • Accessible only to you. You can disconnect any wearable at any time from the app's Settings.

4. AI Processing (Claude API)

Kyronix uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate health insights and parse lab documents. When generating insights, de-identified health data (no name, date of birth, or raw genetic sequences) is sent to Anthropic's API over an encrypted connection. Anthropic's data processing is governed by their privacy policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Health and lab data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage.
  • Genetic data never leaves your device.
  • All API communications use TLS encryption.
  • We do not maintain a central database of your personal health records.

6. Apple HealthKit

Kyronix integrates with Apple HealthKit. Data obtained from HealthKit is not used for advertising, is not shared with third parties without your consent, and is not used for any purpose other than providing health features within the app. This is consistent with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke HealthKit or wearable device permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Delete all app data by deleting the Kyronix app from your device.
  • Contact us to request deletion of any data we may hold.

8. Children's Privacy

Kyronix is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by updating the effective date above.
