FAQ

Is Kyronix a medical provider?

No. Kyronix provides educational insights to support informed decisions. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical care.

Do I need a DNA test to use Kyronix?

No, but adding DNA data improves personalization depth. You can still get value from wearable and lab data.

Is my health data private?

Kyronix is built with privacy-first principles, encryption, and user-controlled data connections.

Can Kyronix tell me which medications are safe?

Kyronix highlights potential considerations and interactions to discuss with your licensed clinician. Medical decisions should always be made with your doctor.
