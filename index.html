FAQ

Common questions.

Is Kyronix a medical provider?

No. Kyronix provides educational insights to support informed decisions. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical care. Always work with your licensed clinician for medical decisions.

Do I need a DNA test to use Kyronix?

No — but adding DNA data improves personalization depth significantly. You can start with just wearable and lab data and add your DNA file anytime. AncestryDNA and 23andMe files are supported.

Is my health data private?

Yes. Kyronix is built with privacy-first principles. Your data is encrypted, you control exactly what you connect, and we never sell your data or use it for advertising.

Can Kyronix tell me which medications are safe?

Kyronix highlights potential genetic considerations and interactions to discuss with your licensed clinician. It provides context, not prescriptions. Medical decisions should always be made with your doctor.
