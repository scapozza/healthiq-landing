FAQ

Common questions

Is Kyronix a medical provider?
No. Kyronix provides educational health insights based on your data. We are not a medical provider and do not offer diagnoses, treatments, or medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.
Do I need a DNA test to use Kyronix?
No — Kyronix works without genetic data and provides personalized insights from your wearables and labs alone. However, connecting your AncestryDNA or 23andMe file significantly enhances personalization across all features.
Is my data private and secure?
Privacy is foundational to Kyronix. We use on-device processing wherever possible, your data is never sold, and you maintain full control over what you share. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
Can Kyronix tell me which medications are safe for me?
Kyronix highlights pharmacogenomic considerations — how your DNA may affect how you process certain medications — and flags potential drug-gene interactions to discuss with your doctor. We surface information, not prescriptions.
